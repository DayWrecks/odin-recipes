Chocolate Cake

Description

It's almost as easy as the boxed stuff, but its so much more delicious. You might not want to calorie count this one though

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups white sugar
  • 1.75 cups all-purpose flour
  • 0.75 cup usweetened cocoa powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 0.5 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two nine inch round pans
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar,flour,cocoa,baking powder and salt. add the eggs, milik, oil and vanilla, mix for two minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last; the batter should be thin
  3. Pour evenly into the prepared pans
  4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, then, cool in the pans for 10 minutes
  5. remove the cake and let it cool
